Specific metabolic maps

This tool allows you to get the metabolic reactions and intermediates that generate your molecule/s of interest as a final product. Not only will you explore the production pathways, but also the detectable molecules linked to them.

Personalized plasmids

Prepare and design your own plasmids in silico. Our model predicts the most suitable genetic sequence to insert in your plasmid for the host microorganism to begin the biomanufacturing process of your target molecule/s. Save time and increase efficiency.
